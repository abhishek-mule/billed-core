## Ravi's Pain Points

### Before BillZo

| Pain | Example |
|------|----------|
| Desktop is slow | "Tally takes 2 minutes to open" |
| Can't bill from phone | "I'm on the shop floor, laptop is inside" |
| Email is slow | "Customer leaves shop without invoice copy" |
| GST anxiety | "What if I made a mistake? CA will shout" |
| No visibility | "Did yesterday's invoices reach Tally?" |
| Accountant dependency | "Need CA for every small thing" |

### What Ravi Wants

1. **Speed** - "30 seconds max per invoice"
2. **Phone** - "My phone is always in my hand"
3. **WhatsApp** - "All my customers are on WhatsApp"
4. **Confidence** - "GST must be correct, accountant shouldn't shout"
5. **Visibility** - "Know what's synced and what's not"
